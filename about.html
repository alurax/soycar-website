Our Story

Our Journey

SoyCar Transport & Car Rentals was founded in 2015 by Mr. Edgar Adoptante, a proud Palawan local who recognized the need for reliable and comfortable transportation for both tourists and residents. What began with a single van making nonstop trips has grown into one of the most trusted transport providers in Palawan.

The name “SoyCar” reflects both the founder’s nickname, “Soy,” and the company’s commitment to dependable travel. Over the years, SoyCar has expanded its fleet and services to meet the growing needs of local and international travelers — all while staying true to its core values of safety, reliability, and excellent customer service.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of customers annually, helping them explore the beautiful island of Palawan with comfort and peace of mind. From airport transfers to custom tours of Palawan's natural wonders, our experienced team ensures every journey is memorable for all the right reasons.

Our Mission & Values

At SoyCar Transport & Car Rentals, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and comfortable transportation services that exceed our customers' expectations while promoting sustainable tourism in Palawan.

Our Core Values:

  • Safety First: The safety of our passengers is our top priority. All our vehicles undergo regular maintenance, and our drivers are professionally trained.
  • Reliability: We understand the importance of punctuality and dependability in transportation services.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We go above and beyond to ensure our customers have a pleasant experience.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We are committed to minimizing our environmental footprint through regular vehicle maintenance and responsible tourism practices.
  • Community Support: As a locally-owned business, we actively participate in community development initiatives and support local employment.
